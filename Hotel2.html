Hotels in Kyoto

Discover your ideal stay in the heart of Japan's cultural capital

Luxury Hotels

Experience traditional elegance and modern comfort at these top luxury hotels:

  • The Ritz-Carlton Kyoto: Riverside luxury with stunning views of the Kamogawa River and classic Japanese design.
  • Hoshinoya Kyoto: A serene ryokan-style resort blending nature and tradition in the Arashiyama district.
  • Four Seasons Hotel Kyoto: Beautiful garden setting with exquisite amenities in the historic Higashiyama area.

Budget Hotels

Comfortable and affordable accommodations for savvy travelers:

  • Guest House An: Cozy and friendly guesthouse close to Kyoto Station.
  • Piece Hostel Kyoto: Modern and social hostel with clean private and dorm rooms.
  • Hotel Mystays Kyoto Shijo: Well-located hotel offering good value for money and easy access to attractions.

Booking Tips

  • Reserve your stay early, especially during cherry blossom season and autumn foliage.
  • Confirm cancellation policies and check if free cancellation is available.
  • Read recent guest reviews on popular booking platforms for up-to-date insights.
  • Consider proximity to Kyoto Station or major subway lines for convenient travel.
  • Try traditional ryokans for an authentic Japanese experience, but book ahead as they fill fast.
