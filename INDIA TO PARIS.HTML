How to Reach Paris from India

Your travel guide to the City of Light

Flight Options

To reach Paris from India, the most convenient way is by air. Here are some tips:

  • Major Airports in India: Delhi (DEL), Mumbai (BOM), Bengaluru (BLR), Chennai (MAA), and Hyderabad (HYD).
  • Destination Airport: Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport (CDG) or Orly Airport (ORY).
  • Direct Flights: Airlines like Air India and Air France offer direct flights from Delhi and Mumbai to Paris.
  • Connecting Flights: Other airlines like Emirates, Qatar Airways, Lufthansa, and British Airways provide connecting flights via their hubs.
  • Typical flight duration: 9 to 11 hours for direct flights.

Visa Information

Before traveling, make sure you have a valid Schengen visa for France:

  • Apply through the official French consulate or visa application centers in India.
  • Required documents typically include a valid passport, travel itinerary, proof of accommodation, travel insurance, and financial proof.
  • Processing time can take up to 15 days, so apply well in advance.

Check the official French Embassy in India website for the latest details.

Travel Tips

  • Book flights early to get better prices, especially during tourist seasons (spring and summer).
  • Consider travel insurance that covers health, trip cancellations, and luggage.
  • Learn some basic French phrases to help with communication.
  • Arrange transportation from the airport to your accommodation (train, taxi, or shuttle).
